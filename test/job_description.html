FAQ’s

Why Join Us?

Instacart is a new age company that provides flexible working hours to its shoppers and allows them to earn easy money. It’s a fun-loving and stress-free job that gives you flexibility and a more exciting environment to work in.

How much time does the delivery drivers take to deliver the order?

They can deliver the order within an hour or two depending upon their availability. The customers also have the opportunity to view available delivery times for their area.  

What items does Instacart not deliver?

At present, it does not deliver alcohol, tobacco, pharmacy prescription, OTC drugs, catering, gift cards, and special order products.
